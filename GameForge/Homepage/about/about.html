About GameForge

Welcome to GameForge, a platform built by a begginer college student named Gurjot. As an newbie web developer, I created GameForge to help gamers discover and explore a wide range of exciting titles. However, please note that **I do not own or sell any of the games featured here**. My website simply redirects users to the original game platforms such as Steam, Epic Games, and others, where the games can be purchased or downloaded.

Our Mission

The mission of GameForge is simple: **to connect gamers with the best games available online**. I aim to provide an easy-to-navigate platform where users can explore top-rated games and then quickly find the correct official platform to download them.
